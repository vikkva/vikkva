Sydney Opera House

The Sydney Opera House is one of the most famous landmarks in Australia. Sydney Opera House featuring distinctive white sail-shaped roofs rising gracefully over the harbor.

Parliament House

Parliament House

The main government building of Australia, located in Canberra. Canberra Parliament House featuring a modern architectural design with a large grass lawn and gardens in front. The building is centered under a clear sky, conveying a formal and welcoming mood.

The Twelve Apostles

The Twelve Apostles

Natural limestone stacks off the coast of Victoria, Australia. The Twelve Apostles are a collection of limestone stacks that rise majestically from the Southern Ocean, showcasing the stunning natural beauty of the Australian coastline.

Brisbane

A modern city with a subtropical climate and a lively riverfront.Brisbane featuring a modern skyline with the Brisbane River in the foreground. The image captures the vibrant atmosphere of the city with clear blue skies and also with massive buildings.

Adelaide

Adelaide

Known for its festivals and nearby wine regions.Adelaide is the capital of the Australian state of South Australia and is the country's fifth largest city. The Adelaide metropolitan area had a population of 1,333,927 in 2017. The city is named after Queen Adelaide.
